### Projects
#### READ-ECG: Signal Reconstruction
As an MSc student at QMUL, I led the development of a novel CNN-Transformer model designed to reconstruct high-fidelity digital signals from paper ECGs. 
* Designed an experimental framework to quantify the performance gap between signal and image domain and validate the superiority of the proposed pipeline on downstream
tasks
* Engineered a scalable pipeline using datasets like PTB-XL and MIMIC-IV-ECG.
